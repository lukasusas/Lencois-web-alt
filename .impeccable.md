# Design Context

- Target audience: Mid-upper and mid-low premium lot buyers in Brazil, primarily Brazilians today, with room to appeal to international tourists and kitesurfers later.
- Use cases:
  - Present the condominium at a high level.
  - Build awareness and desirability before sales formally begin.
  - Help visitors understand the atmosphere, location, and lifestyle around Lençóis.
- Brand personality:
  - Calm
  - Nature
  - Active lifestyle
